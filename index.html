Frequently Asked Questions

What prerequisite knowledge is required?

Basic understanding of programming logic and web fundamentals is recommended. However, our Module 1 is designed to bring everyone up to speed, regardless of their starting point.

Do I need physical hardware?

No. Our platform provides high-fidelity virtual twins of industrial kits. You can practice all exercises directly in your browser using our advanced simulation environment.

How long does the certification take?

Most students complete the full curriculum in 3-4 months studying part-time (10-15 hours per week). The platform is self-paced, so you can adjust to your schedule.
